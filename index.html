Invest in Dubai

The world's most rewarding real estate market. Tax-free income, world-class infrastructure, and unmatched lifestyle — backed by a stable regulatory framework.

Why Dubai in 2025

6 Reasons Dubai
Outperforms Every Market

01

No Property Tax in UAE

Dubai charges no annual property tax and no capital gains tax on your investment. However, Indian residents are required to declare and pay applicable tax on overseas rental income in India.

02

8–12% Gross Rental Yields

Among the world's highest gross yields — far ahead of London (3%), Singapore (3.5%), or Mumbai (2–4%). Even after Indian tax obligations, net returns remain highly competitive.

03

Golden Visa — AED 2M+

Invest AED 2M+ and secure a 10-year UAE residency visa for yourself and your immediate family.

04

1% Monthly Payment Plans

Developer-backed off-plan projects let you invest with just 5–10% upfront. Made for Indian investors.

05

USD-Pegged Currency

The AED is pegged to the USD — a natural hedge against Indian rupee depreciation for Indian investors.

06

RERA-Regulated Market

Escrow-protected transactions and clear freehold ownership laws protect every buyer in Dubai.
